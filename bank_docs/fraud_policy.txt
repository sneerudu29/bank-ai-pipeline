Bank Fraud Detection and Prevention Policy

1. REAL-TIME MONITORING
All transactions are monitored 24/7 using AI systems.
Suspicious patterns trigger automatic alerts.
High-risk transactions may be temporarily blocked.

2. FRAUD THRESHOLDS
Transactions over $5,000 in unusual locations flagged.
3+ declined transactions in 1 hour triggers review.
International transactions require additional verification.

3. CUSTOMER NOTIFICATION
Customers notified within 24 hours of suspected fraud.
Temporary blocks communicated via SMS and email.
Customers can dispute transactions within 60 days.

4. INVESTIGATION PROCESS
All fraud reports investigated within 5 business days.
Customer accounts credited within 10 business days.
Police reports required for losses over $1,000.

5. AI MODEL GOVERNANCE
Fraud detection models reviewed quarterly.
Model accuracy must exceed 95% before deployment.
False positive rate must stay below 2%.
All model changes require security team approval.
