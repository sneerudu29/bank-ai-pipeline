Bank Data Privacy and Protection Policy

1. DATA CLASSIFICATION
Public: Marketing materials, published reports
Internal: Employee communications, procedures
Confidential: Customer data, financial records
Restricted: Authentication data, encryption keys

2. CUSTOMER DATA HANDLING
Customer PII encrypted at rest and in transit.
Access to customer data on need-to-know basis.
Customer data never shared without explicit consent.
Data minimization — collect only what's needed.

3. AI AND MACHINE LEARNING DATA
Training data must be anonymized before use.
No real customer PII in development environments.
Model outputs must not expose customer information.
AI decisions affecting customers must be explainable.

4. DATA RETENTION
Active account data retained for account lifetime.
Closed account data retained for 7 years.
Transaction data retained for 7 years minimum.
Backup data encrypted and stored in Canada only.

5. BREACH RESPONSE
Security breaches reported to OSFI within 72 hours.
Affected customers notified within 24 hours.
Full incident report within 30 days of discovery.
